# Task 1 — Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
This infographic was created using **Canva** (canva.com), a free online graphic design platform that offers a wide range of templates, icons, and styling options ideal for creating visual awareness material.

## What the Infographic Covers
The infographic is a single-page visual resource designed to help batchmates understand the essentials of digital literacy. It covers three key topics:

1. **What Digital Literacy Is** — A clear definition explaining that digital literacy goes beyond just using a computer; it encompasses the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies responsibly.

2. **Useful Digital Tools for Students** — A curated list of essential platforms and tools that every college student should be familiar with, including Google Workspace, GitHub, LinkedIn, and coding practice platforms like HackerRank.

3. **Safe Internet Practices** — Key guidelines on staying safe online, including creating strong passwords, recognising phishing attempts, protecting personal information, and being mindful of one's digital footprint.
